A **multi-tenant marketplace platform** with PCI-DSS compliant payments, SSO authentication, and a microservices architecture designed for massive scale.

---

## The Problem

Launching a marketplace is hard. Payment compliance, multi-vendor logistics, inventory sync, and fraud prevention require specialized expertise. Most startups spend months building infrastructure instead of their actual product.

---

## The Solution

![Microservices Architecture](/images/projects/bazaar/microservices.svg)
*Pre-built services for catalog, orders, inventory, and payments*

---

## How It Works

Each service runs independently with event-driven communication. Sellers onboard through automated KYC/AML verification. The storefront SDK enables white-label deployments in hours, not months.

![Seller Dashboard](/images/projects/bazaar/dashboard.svg)
*Real-time analytics and seller management*

---

## Key Features

- **PCI-DSS compliant** — secure payments out of the box
- **Multi-tenant** — one codebase, infinite storefronts
- **Auto-scaling** — handles flash sale traffic spikes
- **Real-time sync** — inventory across all channels
- **White-label SDK** — your brand, our infrastructure

---

## Who It's For

Startups launching two-sided marketplaces. Enterprises modernizing legacy e-commerce. Anyone who needs marketplace infrastructure without the overhead.

---

## Current Status

Production-ready. Processing transactions across 3 pilot marketplaces. Expanding payment provider integrations and building the seller mobile app.
